OpenAC The Roblox® Anti-Cheat standard
OpenAC is proud to be a leading force in the AntiCheat industry, driven by our passionate team: Liker, Unlimited, and Müsli. We've made a mark in the Roblox® community by crafting invaluable DevForum resources that address various forms of exploiting software. Our work includes detection solutions for tools like Celery, all mobile exploits, and Electron. Additionally, we guide developers on how to enhance their existing anti-exploit systems with our resources on AntiCheat methods and securing your anti-cheat.

Our journey began with the development of notable anti-cheat solutions like ASTON, Karma, LAT-AT, and Polygon AE, which have now ceased to exist. We also played a key role in detecting the now defunct executor Synapse X, a popular exploiting tool that had over 70,000 users, before the acquisition of Byfron Technologies by Roblox® in late 2022.
